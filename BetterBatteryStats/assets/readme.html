v1.5.1.0 2012-10-14

  • Attempt to fix FC for MIUI users

v1.5.0.0 2011-12-29

  • Fixed graphs for Ice Cream Samdwich
  • Dumpfiles are now named with timestamp to avoid overwriting
  • Alarms have been added to dump (for rooted devices)
  • Kernel wakelocks now have references (since unlugged, since charged, custom)
  • Use of Google Analytics to track screen accesses, displayed statitics and stat types. The results help me putting the right priorities in future developments. Opting-out from analytics can be done in "Advances Preferences"
  • "Actions" menu renamed to "More"
  • Added raw Kernel Wakelocks to "More" menu
  • Bugfix: alarms were truncated when upper-case or numbers in the name
  • Bugfix: notify with error message when dumps could not be written due to unmounted sdcard
  • Bugfix: retrieval of online knowledge base was blocking refresh under bad netowrk conditions
  • Bugfix: proper error handling if the battery service could not be attached to (typical MIUI problem)
